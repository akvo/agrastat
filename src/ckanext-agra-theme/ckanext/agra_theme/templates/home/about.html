AGRASTAT serves as a central data hub for African agriculture, developed and maintained by AGRA. Its core purpose is to empower stakeholders within the agriculture sector by providing dependable and easily accessible data and analytics services, ultimately enhancing the precision of policy and investment choices.

Turning Data into Decisions

AGRASTAT aims to transform raw data into valuable information and knowledge, facilitating evidence-based decision-making for sustainably growing Africa's food systems. Currently, the platform offers access to {{ num_datasets }} Datasets across {{ num_countries }} African Countries, covering impact areas such as Production, Nutrition, Markets and Trade, Seed systems, Sustainable farming, and Policy.

Smart Tools for Smart Farming

AGRASTAT is designed with several key features to streamline data access and utilisation. It includes a data hub with data storage, embedded analytics, and user-friendly dashboards for exploring and visualising information. AGRASTAT is an evolving resource, with ongoing development focused on expanding its data offerings and analytical capabilities to support agricultural transformation across Africa.
